Project Overview -
This repository contains a comprehensive data analysis of Astrosage's call center operations aimed at optimizing performance, customer satisfaction, and resource allocation. Leveraging over 28,000+ consultation records, the analysis explores historical trends, operational inefficiencies, workload distribution, and revenue patterns. The goal is to guide strategic investments following the recent â‚¹1 crore funding to enhance customer experience, reduce agent burnout, and increase profitability.

Key Features -
Consultation Trends: Insights into daily consultation volumes, peak hours, and seasonal fluctuations.
Revenue Analysis: Breakdown of revenue sources, emphasizing call consultations as the primary contributor.
Call & Chat Status Distribution: Identification of dropped or incomplete sessions to inform process improvements.
Customer Satisfaction Insights: Evaluation of ratings and feedback patterns, highlighting areas for agent training and customer engagement.
Guru Workload Distribution: Detection of overloaded vs. underutilized agents, providing recommendations for smarter call routing.
Operational Cost Assessment: Monthly cost trends, budget planning, and cost optimization strategies.
Top Performers: Identification of highly rated gurus for recognition and mentorship opportunities.
Investment Recommendations: Structured suggestions including agent hiring, technology upgrades, customer support tools, and real-time dashboards.
Methodology -
Data exploration using Excel Pivot Tables, correlation analysis, and interactive dashboards. Focused on metrics like consultation duration, call completion rates, customer repeat behavior, and satisfaction scores. Actionable suggestions are rooted in statistical insights and aligned with business priorities.

Conclusion -
This analysis equips Astrosage with data-driven insights to strategically allocate investments, improve operational efficiency, and strengthen customer satisfaction. By addressing workload imbalance, optimizing resource distribution, and prioritizing high-impact interventions, this roadmap lays the foundation for sustainable growth and enhanced user experience.
